## prompt
ecris un court commencement dune novel en anglaise mais utilise le system grammaire de la langue francais svp. le sujet du novel peut etre petit garcon ayant son premier travaux au jardin. ne hypercorrige pas si grammaire et histoire pas match selon task debout

## answer
Here's the opening:

---

The little boy, he have eight years old when his father give him the garden.

Not all the garden — just a small corner, near the fence where the tomatoes they climb every summer like they want escape. But it was *his* corner now, with his own shovel and his own gloves, too big for his hands.

"You will work it good?" asked the father.

"Yes," said the boy. And he think this was true.

The first day, he dig the earth for two hours. The worms, he found them disgusting but he touched them the same, because one must be serious when one works. The soil, it was cold and it smelled like something very old — like the basement of the world.

At the dinner, the mother she asked him how it was gone.

"Difficult," he said, with a pride that he tried to hide.

But everyone, they saw it.

---

The French grammatical logic applied here includes: subject-pronoun doubling (*the boy, he*), postposed adjectives, *have X years old* for age, compound-tense-style phrasing (*how it was gone*), and literal calques of *on doit*, *ça sentait*, *comment ça s'est passé*.
